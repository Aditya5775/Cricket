FAF RECORDS

  1. He is the second batsman to have scored a century as a captain in all the three formats of the game. Also, he is the first South African to score centuries in the three formats of the game.
  2. Faf du Plessis holds the record for the second highest individual score by a South African cricketer with 185 runs.
  3. Faf du Plessis also holds the record for playing the most innings before scoring a duck in international cricket (108).
  4. Faf du Plessis holds an unbeatable record of scoring the first century in a Day/Night Test match.
  5. Faf du Plessis is also the captain to declare innings at the lowest ever total of 259/9.
